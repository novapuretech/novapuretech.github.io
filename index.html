// Who we are
The Nova story.

Nova Pure Tech & Internet started as a side hustle with one simple goal: give local homes and businesses access to the kind of IT and internet support that big companies take for granted.

We noticed a gap — small businesses and households in the Bandung area were underserved by generic providers who didn't care about quality or speed of service. So we built something different.

Today, we handle everything from setting up your home Wi-Fi to designing enterprise-level network infrastructure and security systems. Every client gets the same level of attention: full, honest, and fast.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, affordable, and honest technology services to every client — no matter their size.

Our Vision

To become West Java's most trusted local tech and internet infrastructure partner by 2027.

Our Promise

No ghost calls, no hidden fees, no shortcuts. We show up and we solve it — period.
